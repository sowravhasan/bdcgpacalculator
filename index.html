How CGPA is Calculated

CGPA Formula: CGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Σ Credit

Enter each subject's credit hours and grade (letter grade, percentage, or direct GPA), then the calculator automatically computes your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA using the weighted average formula.

Example Calculation:

  • Mathematics: 3 credits × 4.00 GPA = 12.00 grade points
  • Physics: 3 credits × 3.75 GPA = 11.25 grade points
  • Chemistry: 3 credits × 3.50 GPA = 10.50 grade points
  • Total: 9 credits, 33.75 grade points
  • CGPA: 33.75 ÷ 9 = 3.75

Frequently Asked Questions

How is CGPA calculated in Bangladesh universities?

CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Σ(Credit). Bangladesh follows the UGC grading system where A+ = 4.00, A = 3.75, A- = 3.50, and so on. This calculator supports both public universities (DU, BUET, Jahangirnagar) and private universities (NSU, IUB, BRAC).

Which Bangladesh universities are supported by this calculator?

All major Bangladesh universities are supported including: Public Universities - University of Dhaka (DU), BUET, Jahangirnagar University, University of Chittagong, RUET, CUET; Private Universities - North South University (NSU), BRAC University, Independent University Bangladesh (IUB), AIUB, East West University, and 40+ more.

Can I calculate CGPA for multiple semesters at once?
